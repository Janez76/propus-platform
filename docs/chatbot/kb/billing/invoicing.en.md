# Invoicing and Payment

Set up clearly so there's no debate after delivery.

## Invoice issuance

- Invoice goes out **after** delivery, not before.
- Sent digitally by e-mail to the billing address recorded in the order —
  as a PDF with a QR payment slip (Swiss standard).
- Invoice language: German or English, matching the order language.

## Payment terms

Standard payment term: 30 days net from invoice date. On request and for
recurring work, terms can be adjusted.

## Payment methods

- QR invoice (default, readable by any Swiss bank)
- IBAN transfer (shown on the invoice)
- TWINT for amounts under CHF 500 on request

We don't accept credit cards directly — if credit card payment is
required, reach out and we set up a Stripe link.

## Dunning

In case of late payment:

1. **First reminder** 14 days after due date, free.
2. **Second reminder** 14 days later, with a CHF 20 reminder fee.
3. **Third reminder** another 14 days later, with a CHF 40 fee and notice
   of formal collection.
4. After that, handed over to our collections process.

## Billing address different from booker

Allowed. During booking you can record a separate billing address —
typical case: broker books, owner pays. Important: both sides need to
agree, otherwise the invoice goes to the booker.

## Discounts and bundles

- **Bundled jobs:** multiple properties on the same day → travel zone
  charged only once
- **Recurring work:** from the third booking in a quarter, individual
  terms — reach out
- **Package offers:** photo + Matterport + floor plan as a bundle is
  cheaper than the individual bookings — the wizard shows this
  automatically
