# Matterport 3D Tour

A walkable 3D tour of the property. Prospects can move through every room
from any angle, measure distances, and view a floor plan. Ideal for
pre-qualification: on-site viewings afterwards are more serious, because
basic questions are already answered.

## What you get

- Complete 3D walkthrough of the property (all accessible rooms)
- Hosting on the Matterport platform with a private link, embeddable on
  your own website or listing
- Automatically generated 2D floor plan for overview (not a substitute for a
  surveyed floor plan)
- Measurement tool for prospects (distances, room sizes)
- Optional: branding overlay with your logo and contact details

## Capture process

We capture with a Matterport Pro scanner. Per 100 m² we plan around 30
minutes. During the scan the property needs to be empty of people (no motion
in frame), doors open, all rooms accessible.

## Hosting and lifecycle

A Matterport tour is not a one-off deliverable — it sits on a cloud server
and is hosted. Standard runtime covers one marketing phase. After that,
three options:

- **Renewal** (CHF 59) — tour stays online unchanged
- **Reactivation** (CHF 74) — an archived tour is brought back online
- **Archival** — tour goes offline; can be reactivated later if needed

Details see `matterport-lifecycle/lifecycle.en.md`.

## Common follow-ups

- **Outdoor:** Matterport is an interior tool. Outdoor coverage goes through
  photo and/or drone.
- **Multiple storeys:** no issue, staircases are captured along the way
- **Furniture visible?** Yes, anything in the room appears in the tour.
  Empty rooms often work even better in Matterport.
- **Combined with photography and floor plan:** highly recommended — gives
  you a complete marketing stack
