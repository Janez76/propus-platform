# Video and Reels

Motion content for your property — from a short vertical reel for social
media to a longer marketing tour combining drone, interior, and detail
shots. Ivan owns motion production.

## Formats

- **Reel** (vertical, 9:16, 15–30 s) — Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn
- **Short Tour** (horizontal, 16:9, 30–60 s) — websites, listing platforms
- **Full Tour** (16:9, 60–120 s) — premium marketing, with voiceover or
  music, drone, interior, highlights

## What you get

- Edited final cut in the resolution of your choice (up to 4K UHD)
- Royalty-free background music, mood-matched
- Optional: subtitles (DE/EN), logo overlay, contact card at the end
- Delivery as MP4 via Picdrop, standard 3–7 working days after the shoot

## Briefing — required

Unlike photo and Matterport, video depends heavily on the look you want
(pace, mood, audience, platform). Before the shoot we hold a short briefing
call (15–20 min, phone or online):

- Which property, which strengths to highlight?
- Where will the video run (which platform)?
- Mood: warm/homely, modern/cool, premium/exclusive?
- Voiceover or music only?
- Drone component wanted?

We don't produce without a briefing. The briefing slot is scheduled
automatically when you book.

## Common follow-ups

- **With voiceover:** yes, at a surcharge (voice talent and mastering)
- **Multiple versions** (e.g. reel + short tour from the same footage):
  combine in one booking, significantly cheaper than two separate shoots
- **Your own music:** possible if you hold the licence — otherwise we use
  our royalty-free library
- **Drone included?** Not by default. If drone is wanted, book it as an
  additional service — shoots are coordinated jointly.
