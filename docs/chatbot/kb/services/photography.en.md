# Real Estate Photography

High-quality HDR photography for interiors and exteriors. Suitable for sales,
rental, portfolio, and web presence.

## What you get

- 8 to 25 fully retouched images per property, depending on size and booking
  scope
- Each delivered web-optimised (1920 px long edge, sRGB) **and** in full
  print resolution
- Delivery via Picdrop with a private link, ready to push into IDX or
  marketing platforms
- Standard delivery 24–48 h after the shoot

## Capture technique

We shoot mirrorless full-frame (Nikon Z7), exposure brackets, professional
tripod. In post we fuse the brackets through our in-house HDR pipeline into
natural-looking images — no HDR look, no oversaturated colours.

## Preparing the property

For the shoot to run efficiently and the result to land, before we arrive
the property should be:

- fully tidy (personal items removed, kitchens cleared, bathrooms wiped)
- blinds/curtains uniformly set per floor (open or closed, but consistent)
- all lamps functional, bulbs replaced
- exterior areas free of bins, bikes, tools

For occupied properties we plan 30 minutes of on-site prep (light styling,
furniture adjustments). Full home staging is a separate service.

## Common follow-ups

- **Image count on multi-family buildings:** fixed in the booking
- **Twilight shots:** available, requires a separate slot just before sunset
- **Customer image selection:** customer sees a preview in Picdrop and can
  flag favourites — final retouch pipeline then runs on that selection
- **Repeat shoots** (after renovation or tenant change): reduced package
  possible, since exterior images are often reusable
