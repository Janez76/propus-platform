# Floor Plans

To-scale, surveyed floor plans of your property. Clearly readable, formatted
to Swiss real estate marketing standards.

## What you get

- 2D floor plan per storey, black-and-white or colour
- Optional: 3D floor plan (axonometric view) as an additional perspective
- Scale indicator, north arrow, square metres per room, total living area
- Delivered as PNG and PDF, print-ready

## Capture

We survey the property on site. When combined with Matterport, we use the
scan data as a base and refine with measurements — saves time. Stand-alone
booking takes around 30 minutes per 100 m².

## Delivery time

Standard 3–5 working days after capture. Express (24 h) on request, at a
surcharge.

## What we don't deliver

- **Officially surveyed plans** (for building permits, land registry) —
  these require a licensed surveyor.
- **Furniture plans** with detailed interior layouts — available as a
  special service on request.

## Common follow-ups

- **Dimensions visible?** By default only square metres per room. Full
  dimensions (wall lengths, door widths) on request.
- **Multi-family with different units:** each unit as its own plan —
  reflected in the booking scope.
- **Update after renovation:** purely digital adjustments possible if the
  original plan is from us.
