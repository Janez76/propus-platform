# Drone Imagery

Aerial coverage of the property and its surroundings — photo, video, or
both. Strong sales asset for houses, multi-family buildings, and properties
with location appeal (lake, mountain, park).

## What you get

- 5–15 retouched aerial images, depending on booking scope
- Optional: 4K drone video (10–40 s, edited)
- Web-optimised delivery via Picdrop
- Standard delivery 24–72 h after the flight

## Permission framework in Switzerland

Drone flights are governed by FOCA (BAZL) rules and cantonal regulations.
Before every booking we check:

- Location relative to no-fly or controlled zones (e.g. airport proximity,
  nature reserves)
- Minimum distances from people and buildings
- Privacy (visibility of neighbours, third-party properties)
- Weather and visibility conditions

Near major airports (Zurich, Geneva, Basel, Bern, Sion, Lugano) or within
CTR zones, an individual permit is usually required. We obtain it; lead time
is 1–2 weeks.

For flights over occupied multi-family buildings, written consent from
management or ownership must be in place.

## Weather

Drone shoots need wind below ~10 m/s and no continuous precipitation. If
the forecast 24 h before the slot doesn't hold, we reschedule free of
charge (see `operations/weather.en.md`).

## Common follow-ups

- **Can you fly over my house?** Very likely yes — we verify per address
  before confirming the booking.
- **How high?** Standard 30–80 m, depending on subject and regulation.
- **What about neighbouring properties?** We avoid direct overflights of
  third-party property where possible. Images show the neighbourhood as
  context, not in detail.
- **Combined with ground photography:** possible and common in the same
  slot.
