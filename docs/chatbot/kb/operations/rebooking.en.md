# Reschedule and Cancellation

Clear rules so both sides can plan.

## Customer-initiated reschedule

- **more than 48 h before slot:** free, new slot subject to availability
- **24–48 h before slot:** 50 % surcharge on the travel component
- **less than 24 h:** full travel component is owed; the shoot itself can
  move to a later slot

Weather reschedules initiated by Propus are always free (see
`operations/weather.en.md`).

## Customer-initiated cancellation

- **more than 48 h before slot:** free
- **24–48 h before slot:** 50 % of the order value is owed
- **less than 24 h:** full order value is owed

## If the photographer can't make it

We're a small team. In case of illness or unforeseen unavailability, we
reach out immediately and propose a replacement slot — typically within 7
days. No cost to the customer.

## Repeated reschedules without weather reason

If a job is rescheduled more than twice on the customer side, we jointly
assess whether to dissolve and re-book it. An open booking blocks slot
capacity that other customers need.

## When booked via the bot

Reschedules and cancellations cannot be triggered directly in the chat —
write to office@propus.ch or reply to the booking confirmation. The bot
only creates **new** bookings as drafts.
