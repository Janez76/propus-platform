# Weather and Reschedules

Outdoor shoots — photo and drone — depend on weather. We reschedule free of
charge when the weather degrades the job substantively.

## When we reschedule (free of charge)

- **Drone:** continuous precipitation, wind above 10 m/s, fully closed
  cloud cover with no breaks
- **Outdoor photo:** continuous heavy precipitation, no daylight
- **Twilight shots:** if the blue hour is wiped out by dense cloud cover

Indoor work (Matterport, interior photography, floor plan) is
weather-independent and isn't rescheduled — even if outdoor parts of the
same slot fall through. In that case we split the slot across two days.

## Who decides

The photographer makes the call the evening before (for slots before 10
a.m.) or the morning of the shoot. We reach out proactively by e-mail or
phone — you don't have to chase us.

## If the customer wants to reschedule

Reschedules for other reasons (tenant change, short-notice block, renovation
not finished) are possible:

- **more than 48 h before slot:** free, we offer a replacement slot
- **24–48 h before slot:** 50 % surcharge on the travel component
- **less than 24 h:** full travel component is owed; the shoot itself can
  move to a later slot

## Repeated reschedules

For jobs that need more than two weather-driven reschedules, we actively
discuss alternatives — e.g. shooting in light overcast instead of waiting
for blue sky.
