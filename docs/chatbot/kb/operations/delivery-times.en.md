# Delivery Times

Delivery time starts **on the day of the shoot**, not the day of booking.
Standard times below count from shoot end:

| Service | Standard | Express |
|---|---|---|
| Photography | 24–48 h | 12 h on request |
| Drone photos | 24–72 h | 24 h on request |
| Drone video (edited) | 3–7 working days | 48 h on request |
| Matterport 3D tour | 24–48 h after scan | – |
| Floor plan | 3–5 working days | 24 h on request |
| Reel | 2–4 working days | 48 h on request |
| Short tour | 3–5 working days | – |
| Full tour | 5–10 working days | – |

## When it's urgent

Express delivery is usually doable, at a surcharge and subject to capacity.
For tight deadlines, mention it at the start of the booking — we'll check
capacity in the running week and commit.

## Weekends and holidays

Delivery times count working days (Monday–Friday). A Friday shoot
typically delivers on Tuesday. By special arrangement we also work
weekends (surcharge).

## Delivery format

All digital results land in a Picdrop folder with a private link, which you
receive by e-mail. From there files can be downloaded individually or in
bulk, pulled into your own tools, and shared with third parties.
