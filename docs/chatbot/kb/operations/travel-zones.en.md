# Travel Zones

Propus works nationwide across Switzerland. Travel time and distance flow
into the final price via a zone system. The actual zone is derived
automatically from the property address — it isn't estimated, and it isn't
negotiable.

## How it works

- Starting point is Zug (HQ).
- For each job address, distance and travel time are computed.
- The address falls into one of several zones.
- Each zone carries a fixed surcharge, independent of the specific booking.

Zone assignment and surcharge are computed automatically during booking and
shown transparently before you confirm.

## What the zone does NOT determine

- Capture quality — same team, same equipment, everywhere.
- Delivery time — starts at the shoot, not at travel.
- Availability of drone, Matterport, or video — all available in every
  zone.

## Bundled jobs

Multiple properties on the same day in the same region: the travel zone is
charged only once, plus a small switch surcharge per additional address.
This is significantly cheaper than separate bookings — ask explicitly when
planning a portfolio shoot.

## Binding calculation

The logic described here is conceptual. The **concrete** surcharge for your
address comes from the price estimate in the chat or in the booking wizard
on booking.propus.ch — both compute through the same server-side endpoint.
