# About Propus

Propus GmbH is a B2B provider of visual content for real estate, based in
6300 Zug. We work nationwide across Switzerland for brokers, developers,
property managers and private sellers.

Our offering covers the full visual needs of a marketing campaign:
high-quality interior and exterior photography, Matterport 3D tours, aerial
imagery via drone, short-form video and reels, and to-scale floor plans.
All services can be booked individually or as a bundle.

What sets us apart from mass-market providers: each property is handled by
the same team end-to-end — capture, edit, post-production. No middlemen, no
external subcontractors. Delivery times are committed during booking and
held.

Working languages: German (High German), English. Serbian on request.
